# Finish Taxonomy

Use this reference when choosing or describing print finishes.

## Common Finishes

| Finish | Real visual behavior | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| Spot UV / 局部UV | Clear glossy varnish over existing print; reveals itself through angle-dependent highlights and slightly deeper contrast | White paint, wet glue drops, full-area glare, changing text color |
| Raised UV / 厚UV | Clear raised varnish with rounded micro-edge highlights; best on logos, patterns, icons | Excessive height, jelly-like blobs, imprecise bleed |
| Hot foil / 烫金烫银 | Metallic directional reflection, crisp edges, slight pressed impression | Pure yellow fill, glowing neon, noisy glitter everywhere |
| Emboss / 击凸 | Shallow raised surface, edge highlight and opposite shadow, follows substrate | Detached 3D letters, thick plastic sticker |
| Deboss / 压凹 | Subtle recessed impression and soft inner shadow | Black outlines, holes, exaggerated engraving |
| Matte varnish / 哑油 | Low sheen, clean diffuse surface | Dirty grey haze |
| Gloss varnish / 亮油 | Broader glossy reflection than spot UV if applied over an area | Mirror-like plastic unless specified |
| Soft-touch / 触感膜 | Velvety low-gloss smoothness | Furry texture, rubber look |
| Texture varnish / 纹理油 | Fine tactile pattern visible under grazing light | Random dirt/noise |
| Holographic / 镭射 | Angle-dependent rainbow shift | Full rainbow wash unless requested |

## Intensity Language

- **Subtle**: premium, realistic, mostly visible at highlight angles.
- **Medium**: visible in normal product photography, still believable.
- **Strong**: showpiece render; use only when the user asks for obvious process display.

## Bottle & Container Body Finishes (3D Surface)

These are applied directly to the bottle/container body, not to flat labels.

| Finish | Real visual behavior | Compatible materials | Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| 喷涂 Matte Spray | 均匀哑光表面，微颗粒触感，柔和低反光 | Glass, PET, Acrylic, Aluminum | 喷涂不均/流挂/橘皮纹 |
| 喷涂 Glossy Spray | 高亮光滑表面，镜面级反光，色彩饱和 | Glass, PET, Acrylic, Aluminum | 过度反光导致看不清瓶型 |
| 橡胶漆/手感漆 Soft-touch Spray | 天鹅绒般哑光弹性触感，极低反光，指纹不明显 | PET, PP, Acrylic, Aluminum | 过于粗糙像砂纸、剥落感 |
| 渐变喷涂 Gradient Spray | 底部到顶部颜色渐深或渐浅，自然无断层 | Glass, PET, Acrylic | 断层线、颜色过渡不自然 |
| 电镀/真空镀 Electroplating | 镜面金属光泽，高反射，可做铬/金/玫瑰金色 | Glass, PET, Acrylic (需底涂) | 廉价塑料电镀感、橘皮、氧化斑 |
| 蒙砂/磨砂 Frosting | 半透明雾面，光线散射柔化，玻璃特有质感 | Glass only | 不均匀雾度、划痕、指纹残留 |
| 瓶身丝印 Screen on Bottle | 油墨直接印在瓶身曲面，薄层、边缘清晰 | Glass, PET, PP, Aluminum | 丝印偏移、油墨过厚浮起、不耐刮 |
| 瓶身烫金/烫银 Hot Stamp on Bottle | 金属箔热压转印到曲面，镜面光泽，微凹痕 | Glass (需底涂), PET, Acrylic | 烫印不牢、边缘毛刺、曲面失真 |
| 烤花/贴花 Decal Transfer | 高温烧结图案融入玻璃/陶瓷表面，永久附着 | Glass, Ceramic | 贴花边缘白膜、起泡、颜色暗沉 |
| 激光雕刻 Laser Engraving | 精细蚀刻图案，微米级深度，哑光质感 | Glass, Acrylic, Aluminum, Wood cap | 雕刻过深像破损、边缘烧焦感 |
| 高光注塑 High-gloss Mold | 模具直接成型的高光表面，无需二次处理 | PP, PE, PET, Acrylic | 注塑流痕、缩水、熔接痕 |
| 亚光注塑 Matte Mold | 模具晒纹/蚀纹直接成型的哑光表面 | PP, PE, PET, Acrylic | 纹理不均匀、脱模拉伤 |

## Bottle + Label Relationship Constraints

- 瓶身喷涂区域与标签区域需提前规划：标签不干胶在喷涂面上的附着力可能不足，需预留净瓶区域或使用底涂
- 收缩套标可覆盖喷涂瓶身，但套标收缩后的纹理需与瓶身工艺协调
- 丝印直接印在瓶身上时，瓶身工艺（喷涂/电镀）作为底色，丝印油墨需与之兼容
- 透明/半透明瓶身（蒙砂玻璃、透明PET）的标签设计需考虑瓶内液体颜色/透明度的影响

## Flexible Packaging Finishes (Soft Pouches & Bags)

These are applied to flexible film/laminate packaging. All finishes must respect the flexible nature of the material — rigid box processes like heavy emboss/deboss do not apply.

| Finish | Real visual behavior | Compatible with | Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| 哑油/亮油对比 Matte/Gloss Varnish | 大面积哑面 + 局部亮光区域对比，同一平面层次分明 | All laminates | 亮油区域过大导致廉价塑料感 |
| 局部 UV on Film | 透明亮光涂层覆盖特定图案，角度依赖高光 | PET/PE, MATT OPP, 铝箔复合 | 油墨过厚导致薄膜硬化开裂 |
| 低温烫金/烫银 Cold/Hot Foil on Film | 金属箔热压到薄膜表面，镜面光泽，微凹痕 | PET/PE, OPP/CPP, MATT OPP | 高温烫穿薄膜、烫印区域起皱 |
| 镭射膜/全息膜 Holographic Film | 基膜自带全息彩虹光泽，角度变化色彩流动 | 作为复合层嵌入 | 整袋满版镭射过于刺眼，宜局部 |
| 镀铝膜 Metallized Film | 银色铝层蒸镀在基膜上，金属光泽 | 作为复合层嵌入 | 与烫银同时使用层次混乱 |
| 拉链封口 Zipper / Resealable Strip | 凹凸扣拉链或滑块拉链焊在袋口内侧 | 自立袋、三边封袋、八边封袋 | 拉链颜色与袋身设计冲突 |
| 吸嘴+盖 Spout with Cap | 塑料吸嘴焊接在袋顶，带旋盖或翻盖 | 自立袋（吸嘴袋） | 吸嘴比例与袋身不协调 |
| 易撕口 Tear Notch | V型缺口或激光划线在热封边上方，方便撕开 | 所有袋型 | 位置不明显导致找不到、撕裂线歪斜 |
| 挂孔 Hang Hole / Euro Slot | 顶部冲孔，用于挂钩陈列 | 自立袋、三边封袋 | 冲孔太靠近热封边导致撕裂 |
| 开窗 Window | 透明膜区域让消费者看到内容物 | 铝箔袋、牛皮纸复合袋 | 开窗边缘热封不牢导致漏气 |
| 压纹 on Film | 薄膜表面压出纹理，触感和视觉立体感 | PET/PE, OPP/CPP | 纹理过深导致薄膜减薄破裂 |
| 激光打码/喷码 Laser/Inkjet Coding | 生产日期/批号印在热封边或背面 | 所有袋型 | 打码位置覆盖主视觉区域 |

## Flexible Packaging Physical Constraints

- 热封边（通常宽度 5-10mm）是工艺禁区：烫金/UV/丝印不得跨越热封区域，否则影响密封强度
- 自立袋底部折叠面（bottom gusset）和侧面风琴（side gusset）在展开/折叠状态视觉差异大，效果图应展示装满内容物后的自然膨胀形态
- 柔性材料无法做击凸/压凹/厚重UV — 只能用哑油/亮油对比、烫金、局部UV、镭射膜来营造层次
- 袋子自然褶皱和反光是柔性包装的真实特征，过度平滑会像硬盒而非软袋
- 吸嘴/拉链等硬质附件与软质袋身的交接处需有自然的热封焊接过渡

## Quality Checks

- Finish must sit on the same perspective plane or curved surface as the packaging.
- Finish should not cover small text unless specifically requested.
- Gloss direction should match the scene light and object curvature.
- Metallic effects should have dark and bright zones, not uniform flat color.
- **Bottle-specific**: Bottle body finish must follow the 3D curvature of the bottle; spray gradient must wrap naturally around cylindrical/conical surfaces; electroplating reflection must bend with the bottle contour.
- **Flexible packaging-specific**: Finishes must follow the natural folds and slight creases of the flexible film; rigid emboss/deboss effects are not physically possible on soft pouches; zipper/spout/cap must look like welded attachments, not floating elements; heat seal edges must remain clean and unprinted.
