# Material Compatibility

Use this reference when matching packaging substrates and finishes.

## Labels

- **Silver dragon PET / 银龙 PET 标签**: satin metallic silver film, cool grey pearlescent reflection, fine film texture. Good with spot UV, raised UV, white ink, black/orange process ink. Avoid making the whole label chrome-mirror unless requested.
- **White PET / 白色 PET**: clean durable plastic label; supports gloss/matte contrast, spot UV, metallic ink.
- **Clear PET / 透明 PET**: needs bottle contents/background awareness; white ink underprint may be required for readability.
- **Paper label**: supports foil, emboss, deboss, varnish; texture should be fibrous and lower reflectivity than PET.

## Paper Packaging

- **Specialty paper / 特种纸**: visible fiber or tactile texture, restrained ink absorption, elegant matte behavior. Good with foil, blind emboss/deboss, spot UV contrast.
- **Coated paper / 铜版纸**: smoother, better gloss and foil precision.
- **Kraft paper / 牛皮纸**: warm brown, rough fiber; foil and black ink work well, fine color accuracy less reliable.

## Plastic / Bottle / Cap

- Keep bottle/cap material separate from label material.
- White HDPE/PP bottles are usually soft matte to semi-gloss, not metallic.
- Ribbed caps need fine vertical grooves preserved; do not smooth them away.

## Practical Pairings

- Silver PET + spot UV: good for technology, aquatic, medical, supplement, and clean premium looks.
- Specialty paper + foil + emboss: good for premium food, tea, cosmetics, gift boxes.
- Matte film + spot UV: good for strong gloss/matte contrast.
- Clear PET + white ink + spot UV: good for transparent bottles where label should float cleanly.
